About Us

Reimagining Plastic Waste

At PlasticUrban, we're pioneering the transformation of plastic waste into durable, sustainable urban infrastructure. Our innovative approach tackles two critical global challenges simultaneously: plastic pollution and urban development needs.

Through our website, we connect with public and industries to help reduce platic waste and promote sustainable living.

Our vision is a world where plastic waste is viewed not as a problem, but as a valuable resource for creating sustainable cities of the future.

The Ocean Crisis

Every year, approximately 8 million metric tons of plastic enter our oceans. This is equivalent to dumping truck of plastic into the ocean every minute

By 2050, there could be more plastic in the ocean than fish by weight if current trends continue.

‚è±

Decomposition Timeline

Plastic bottles take up to 450 years to decompose, while plastic bags take 10-20 years, and plastic straws take up to 200 years.

Much of the plastic ever produced still exists in some form today, as it doesn't biodegrade but rather breaks down into smaller microplastics.

üåé

Global Production

Since the 1950s, about 8.3 billion metric tons of plastic have been produced globally. Only 9% has been recycled, 12% has been incinerated, and 79% has accumulated in landfills or the natural environment.

üß¨

Health Impacts

Microplastics have been found in human blood, placentas, and even breast milk. These tiny particles can carry harmful chemicals and may potentially pose health risks.

Sustainable Solutions

Converting plastic waste into durable infrastructure can extend the lifecycle of plastic materials by 50+ years, diverting them from landfills and creating valuable community assets.

Plastic lumber and composite materials made from recycled plastic can outperform traditional materials in many applications, with greater durability and resistance to weathering.

Individual Impact

Simple actions like using reusable bags, water bottles, and containers can prevent hundreds of plastic items from entering the waste stream annually per person.

Supporting businesses and initiatives that repurpose plastic waste helps create market demand for recycled materials and innovative solutions.
